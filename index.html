The Team

Jacqueline Blackwell, Ph.D., P.Geo.

Principal Consulting Geologist and President

Jacqueline has a Ph.D. in volcanology and economic geology from the University of Tasmania (CODES), a B.Sc. in Earth and Ocean Science from the University of Victoria, and 14 years in the exploration industry. She is a registered Professional Geologist with the Engineers and Geoscientists British Columbia (EGBC) and the Professional Geoscientists Ontario (PGO).
Jacqueline is a specialist in recognition and classification of volcanic and hydrothermal lithofacies in altered and mineralized terranes with exploration experience in orogenic gold, porphyry copper-gold, and epithermal gold deposits.

Tim Stubley, B.Sc.(Hons)

Principal Consulting Geologist and Vice President

Tim got his start in the exploration industry 18 years ago as a field-hand on projects in northern Quebec and central British Columbia, Canada, and in 2010 completed a B.Sc. with Honours in Geology from the University of Tasmania. He is currently undertaking a modular-based Masters in Mineral Exploration at Laurentian University.
Tim specializes in exploration project management including mapping and drilling programs in brownfields and remote helicopter-supported field settings.
